QuickCanvas

QuickCanvas is a student productivity browser extension for Canvas LMS. It helps students stay on top of due dates with a cleaner dashboard, reminders, and optional Google Calendar sync.

Canvas-Focused Designed specifically for Canvas courses and assignments.
Reminder Automation Automatically syncs upcoming work and notification timing.
User-Controlled Calendar access is optional and can be turned off anytime.
